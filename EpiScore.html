Example Script:

Call the function from terminal:

Rscript EpiScore.R --methFolder data/GS_DNAm \
--lassoCoef result/LASSO_weights_MDD \
--out result/episcore_mdd
Rscript EpiScore.R --methFolder mval_test \
--lassoCoef LASSO_weights_MDD \
--out episcore_mdd
